About Us

Nuts and Bolts Est. 2020

Founder John Crider, whose whereabouts are unknown to the public, was a computer science teacher for most of his career as far as the writer of this segment knows. One day in 2020, however, Crider decided "screw it, hardware store." And thus, Nuts and Bolts was born. Crider focuses his business approach on customer satisfaction, adding many features to his website early on allowing customers leave feedback to the company, such as the Testimonials page. To create this web page, Crider would need the help of roughly 7 college students, all of which needed to complete a capstone project to graduate with an associate's degree. He enlisted the help of Billy McGee, Kyle Boldman, Garrett Nye, David Hill, William Johnoff, Dellali Attipoe and CJ Beachler to create such a website. Working for twelve weeks, the students produced the website which you view right now, slowly adding features to the site with each passing week. At the end of the twelve weeks, the site was left as is. Nuts and Bolts continues to prosper to this day as a result of these developments.
